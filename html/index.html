About Me

I’m a Denver based UX/UI designer who combines a love for connecting with people, strengths as an educator, and passion for the important issues facing our world to create user-based innovative creations. I received my Masters of the Arts in science curriculum and instruction design and spent five years working as a science teacher. I found myself wanting to follow new inspiration and enrolled myself in the Bloc Design program where I spent 8 months learn design UX/ UI best practices, visual design, and fronte-end skills from professionals in the field.

When I am not designing, you can find me rock climbing in Colorado’s beautiful canyons.

My Process

“Iteration is the key to innovation.”
  • Research

    Defining the problem, collecting and analyzing user research, and analyzing the competitive marketplace.

  • Information Architecture

    Identifying user stories and flows, creating wireframes, and conducting usability tests.

  • Visual Design

    Creating sketches, identifying the brand identity, conducting preference tests, crafting the style guide and high fidelity mock-ups.

  • Develop

    Developing functional, high fidelity mock-ups using the HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript needed for the solution and deploying these within a testing environment.
